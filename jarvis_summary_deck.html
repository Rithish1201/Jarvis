Core Capabilities

🖥️ Real-Time Monitoring

Live WebSocket tracking of machine health, temperature, vibration, and RPM.

🔮 Predictive Maintenance

AI predicts failures based on sensor trends to schedule maintenance proactively.

🤖 JARVIS AI Assistant

Multi-lingual conversational AI (English, Tamil, Tanglish) with Voice Commands.

🏭 Commissioning Advisor

Data-driven recommendations for setting safe limits on new machines.

Intelligence Layer

  • Statistical Anomalies (Z-Score): Monitors real-time streams against historical baselines.
  • Threshold Detection (μ ± kσ): Computes precise safe operating boundaries based on empirical data.
  • Weighted Health Scoring: Multi-variable fusion (Temp + Vibration + RPM) into a unified 0-100% index.
  • LLM NLP (Ollama/Mistral): Context-aware intent parsing and response generation locally.
